Device gap. Desktop converts at 25.6% vs mobile's 10.5% — a 2.4x gap — even though mobile drives 46% more page views (2,773 vs 1,905). This is the single largest lever in the dataset: fixing mobile checkout UX would likely outperform any channel-spend reallocation.
Channel mix. Social (18.5%) and email (17.0%) outconvert paid ads (15.4%) and referral (14.8%) despite lower traffic volume — organic and paid ads bring the most viewers but convert below the channel average.
Trend. Weekly cohort conversion holds steady in a 12–19% band for six months with no clear drift — the gaps above are structural, not the result of a recent regression.
